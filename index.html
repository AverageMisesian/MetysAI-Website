MetysAI is a reverse engineering assistant that combines Radare2 and ChatGPT to help you understand, debug, and analyze compiled binaries more easily. Whether you're a professional RE analyst or a student learning binaries — MetysAI gives you interactive AI-driven insight into disassembly.
